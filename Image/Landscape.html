'A Landscape 212th', Photograph, 534KB

'The landscape outside the window went through 212 processes and became a pale blue landscape, and the body became lighter than anyone else.'

This process involved opening landscape images taken during a journey on the computer screen, then repeating the cycle by capturing the image again with a phone. Even in the simple process of capturing an image with a phone and transferring it back to the computer, information within the image continues to be lost, and the body gradually becomes lighter.
