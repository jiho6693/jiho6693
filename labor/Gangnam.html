Worked as a Director/CFO. "Love Tour Depot_Gangnma Pavilion", Seoul, 2021



The Love Your Depot Gangnam Pavilion is a public art project that opened in Seoul's Gangnam District in 2021. The "Love Your Depot" project, initiated in 2019 by the National Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art, serves as a warehouse system for art pieces proposed by Jewyorhii.
This special storage facility in Gangnam District, known as Love Your Depot, is a practical storage space for numerous artworks that would otherwise need to be stored
